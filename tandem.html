Ready for a Tandem jump?

Everything you need to know about Tandem jumping

Intro

Tandem is the easiest and most common way to experience a skydive. After a short briefing, you leave the aircraft attached to an instructor. You will then experience an approximately 30sec of wonderful freefall, after which he will open the parachute and brings you to a safe landing while you enjoy the wonderful view during descent under parachute. After a short briefing instructor may also be willing to let you control the parashute during the descent. The descent itself takes about 2-3 minutes.

Description

Tandem skydiving is a very popular training method for first time skydivers, but it is more expensive than a static line. It exposes first-time jumpers to skydiving with minimal expectations from the student. The training may consist of many of the activities performed by any skydiving student, for example, how to exit the aircraft, how to do maneuvers in freefall, and how to deploy the main canopy themselves. However, the tandem master remains primarily responsible for safe and timely [parachute] deployment. Tandem skydiving is very safe, with a student fatality rate of one per 500,000 tandem jumps over the past ten years

Requirements

  • Minimum age 12, max weight 95kg.
  • Over the age of 75, a medical certificate is required.
  • Ask your local dropzone the possibilities in case you have personal disabilities.
