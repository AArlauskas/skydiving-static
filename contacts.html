Goals

Our mindset

Skydiving: it's the original badass sport. When you make that first jump from thousands of feet (sending your awesome-ometer through the roof in the process), you want to make more of a statement than a simple thrilled scream, right?

Our goal is to expand the boundaries of the sport by inviting people from all around the world to join us.

We trully hope that more and more people would see this sport not only as a one time crazy experience (which of course it is), but also as an activity, that might very well change your life.

We are more than welcome to hear from you what you think about skydving! Maybe you are just a begginer who is just getting into the sport or maybe you are already a profesional, looking for like-minded people. Either way, don't be discouraged and contact us!
